Colour

3D Model

From the beginning I wanted a tree as my 3D model that contrasted the colours of a normal tree, giving it an ethereal feeling. The tree was initially going to be pink with blue leaves or vice versa (reminiscent of cherry blossoms), but I felt that it clashed with the minimalistic theme I was going for so I scrapped the pink aspect and replaced it with the final colour theme of silvery brown/ blue. I chose these colours because they are similar enough to real trees such as silver birches as well as being definitively different

website colour

For my website's colour palette, I chose a neutral off-white/cream colour as the base so it would be easy to use and read as well be classic and neutral. I also feel that this colour excentuates and contrasts with the stark blacks and blues of my naviagtion bar and font colour. I wish I had taken more time to refine this idea however as I feel it could have looked a lot better with more aspects and things to look at.
